About Me

Shih-Hung Chiu graduated from National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2019 with a B.S. in Civil Engineering and obtained an M.S. in Systems Engineering, Civil and Environmental Engineering from the University of California, Berkeley in 2022. He is currently a PhD student in Civil Systems under Professor Kenichi Soga. His research projects focus on improving infrastructure resiliency by using advanced sensing technologies and computational modeling.

Research interests:

  • Smart Infrastructure
  • Infrastructure Health Monitoring
  • Distributed Fiber Optic Sensing Technique
  • City-scale Infrastructure Resilience Modeling
